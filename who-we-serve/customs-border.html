The ground reality

Three evidentiary gaps at India's borders.

Published BSF, DRI and DPDP data — not our pitch. These are the operational realities that BWC directly addresses at every checkpoint and patrol line.

01
6,386 km
Land border — patrolled without a footage record

BSF patrols the entire India–Bangladesh and India–Pakistan land perimeter. Encounters, alerts and seizures along this line are documented by radio log and oral debrief — with no video record to anchor the chain of custody.

BSF Annual Report · MHA
02
₹12,000 Cr+
Contraband seized annually — evidence challenged in court

DRI and CBIC seize over ₹12,000 crore in contraband each year. Without GPS-stamped field video, seizure chains are reconstructed from memory — and routinely challenged at the first cross-examination.

DRI / CBIC Annual Report · Ministry of Finance
03
2023
DPDP Act — border data is now a legal obligation

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 applies to all data collected at the border, including biometrics, vehicle records and checkpoint footage. Every camera deployment must be data-sovereign by architecture.

DPDP Act 2023 · Ministry of Electronics & IT
ProSight M530 · the real specification

The hardware that makes evidence stick in court.

  • 2K video (2304×1296) with a 30-second pre-record buffer — seizure context captured even before the officer activates recording.
  • 12-hour rated battery (4500 mAh). Expect ~10 hours real-world with live streaming — covers a full border shift on one charge.

  • ProSight OS. No third-party firmware, no third-party data path. Border footage never leaves ProSight's sovereign stack.
