ProSight M530 · what we can honestly offer

Incident-triggered record. Nothing more.

  • Incident-mode capture. The M530 carries a 30-second pre-record buffer — staff activate only at incident onset; continuous recording is not the default.
  • 2K video (2304×1296) · 4G LTE + Wi-Fi + GPS · AES-256 on-device.
  • 12-hour rated battery (~10h real-world) · 128 GB internal storage.
  • AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) — 100% Indian data residency. DPDP-compliant by architecture.
  • Role-based vault: Medical Superintendent, Nursing Head, Security Head — each sees only their scope.
  • On the honest roadmap: on-device face blur and medical-clip retention policies are Phase-2 capabilities, not live today.
