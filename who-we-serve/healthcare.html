ProSight M530 · what we can honestly offer today

Incident-triggered record. Nothing more.

  • Incident-mode capture. The M530 carries a 30-second pre-record buffer — staff activate only at incident onset. Continuous ward recording is not the default and is not our recommendation.
  • 2K video (2304×1296) · 4G LTE + Wi-Fi + GPS · AES-256 on-device encryption from the first frame.
  • 12-hour rated battery (4500 mAh). Expect ~10 hours real-world — a full hospital shift on one charge.
  • 128 GB internal storage. Auto-upload to AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) — 100% Indian data residency, DPDP Sections 4 & 7 compliant by architecture.
  • Role-based vault. Medical Superintendent, Nursing Head, Security Head — each role sees only their defined scope. No cross-department access.
  • On the honest roadmap: on-device face blur and medical-clip retention policies are Phase-2 capabilities — not live today. We will not ship them until they are right.
