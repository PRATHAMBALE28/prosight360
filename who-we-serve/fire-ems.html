ProSight M530 · what we deliver today

One honest specification.

  • 2K video (2304×1296) with 30-second pre-record buffer.
  • 12-hour rated battery (~10h real-world).
  • 4G LTE + Wi-Fi + GPS. Live stream where cellular is available.
  • 128 GB internal · AES-256 encryption · auto-upload to AWS Mumbai.
  • IP67 + MIL-STD-810G. IR LEDs · 10 m low-light vision.
  • Note: M530 is rated for routine duty environments — it is not a certified structural-fire-entry device. Deploy on Incident Commanders, vehicle crews, recce teams and NDRF logistics wings.
