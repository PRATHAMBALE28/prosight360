Fire stations — zero footage mandate in India today

India operates over 5,000 fire stations through state fire services. Not one national regulation today mandates a video record of incident response — after-action reviews run entirely on radio logs and crew memory.

ProSight M530 · what we deliver today

One honest specification. No overclaims.

  • 2K video (2304×1296) with 30-second pre-record buffer — incident context captured before activation.
  • 12-hour rated battery (4500 mAh). Expect ~10 hours real-world, covering a full response shift on one charge.
  • 4G LTE + Wi-Fi + GPS. Live stream to the control room wherever cellular is available.
  • 128 GB internal · AES-256 encryption · auto-upload to AWS Mumbai. 100% Indian data residency.
  • IP67 + MIL-STD-810G. IR LEDs · 10 m low-light vision. Rated for outdoor and vehicular duty environments.
  • Important: M530 is rated for routine duty environments — it is not a certified structural-fire-entry device. Deploy on Incident Commanders, vehicle crews, recce teams and NDRF logistics wings — not inside a burning structure.
