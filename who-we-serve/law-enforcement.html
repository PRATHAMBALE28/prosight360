ProSight M530 · what you get

One device. Real specifications. No marketing fiction.

  • 2K video (2304×1296) with a 30-second pre-record buffer — no critical moment is ever lost, even before activation.
  • 12-hour rated battery (4500 mAh). Expect ~10 hours of real-world duty use with live streaming active — a full shift on a single charge.
  • 4G LTE + Wi-Fi + GPS — continuous live stream to Command Center wherever the officer has cellular signal.
